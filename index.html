Karina Palmitesta is an editor and writer living in Toronto, Ontario, Canada.

She's edited for print and web magazines, boutique and trade publishers, and non-profit arts organizations. Karina also co-hosts a podcast on linguistic and social change; is the administrative director of Literistic, a deadline service for writers; and coordinates publications for Hot Docs, North America's largest documentary film festival.

Get in touch for professional services in proofreading, copy editing, fact checking, developmental editing, writing, and arts admin.
