THE MOST UNDERRATED BAR YOU KNOW

The Beer Store (SPEC)


Problem. The Beer Store wants to amp up sales to customers in their 30s.

Insight. When you're in your 20s, any bar will do. But in your 30s, you're choosier about how you spend your limited free time.

Solution. Recast the Beer Store as the most underrated bar in Ontario.
