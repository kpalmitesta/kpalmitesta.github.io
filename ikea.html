MAKE IT IKEA OFFICIAL

IKEA (SPEC) — 1st place winner in live client pitch


Problem. Gen Z Canadians aren't visiting IKEA in-store as much as they used to.

Thought starter. Gen Z take a pragmatic approach to love. They're slow to enter committed relationships.

Insight. You wouldn't go to IKEA with someone you weren't serious about.

Solution. If you want to turn your situationship into a relationship, just pop the question: “Will you go to IKEA with me?”
