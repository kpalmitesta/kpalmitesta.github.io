ONTARIO IS AWAKE

CUPE (SPEC)


Backstory. This campaign takes place in an alternate history in which CUPE, Unifor, and other major unions join forces for a general strike in Ontario on November 14, 2022. In this fictional timeline, Doug Ford doubles down on Bill 28, an unprecedented piece of anti-worker legislation with far-reaching consequences.

Problem. A general strike means economic activity in the province grinds to a halt. CUPE has no idea how long or how ugly this will get. They need to get all Ontarians on their side, and quickly.

Insight. People never thought a general strike would happen. Now that it has, they're caught up in the momentâ€”and surprised by their own collective power.

Solution. Ford poked a sleeping giant. Now Ontario is awake. This campaign uses bright, sunny imagery to evoke the dawning of a new era of the labour movement in Ontario.
